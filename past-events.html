Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, TANN, was held on August 23-25, 2017, in Toronto, Canada. Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Mahtab Asadian
    for the paper entitled: " Effects of a Dielectric Barrier Discharge (DBD) Treatment on Chitosan/Polyethylene Oxide Nanofibers and Their Cellular Interactions"
  • Dr. Peng Chen
    for the paper entitled: "Photoluminescence Properties of InGaN/AlGaInN MQWs with Lattice Matched AlGaN/InGaN Superlattices Barrier at Highly Excited Condition"
  • Dr. Tao Tao
    for the paper entitled: " Plasmonic laser using InGaN/GaN Nanorods with Different Shapes"
