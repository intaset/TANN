Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Srinivas Bathini
    for the paper entitled: " Gold Nano-Island Platform for the Microfluidic Plasmonic Detection of Exosomes"
  • Dr. Shaista Taimur
    for the paper entitled: "Synthesis and Characterization of Polystyrene Grafted Nanohybrids by Graft Polymerization"
  • Dr. Narinder Kaur
    for the paper entitled: " Realization of High Aspect Ratio and Surface Defects in YZO Nanorods via Controlling Seed Layer Thickness"
